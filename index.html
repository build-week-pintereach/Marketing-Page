Do you like research?

Do you ever come accross an article you want to read, but don't have the time to read it, or get sidetraced with other research? Pintereach is a new way of keeping track of your research by storing article links in Bins.

＋

What is a Bin and how does it work?

It's simple! Hit the plus to add your article links and click the bins to access your saved articles!

Who is Pintereach for?

Pintereach is for everyone! Pintereach is perfect for students doing research papers, journalists researching the next breaking story, for hobbyists learning a new skill and anyone else looking to stay organized.
